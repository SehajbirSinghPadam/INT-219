Connecting Communities for Inclusive Mobility

"Helping Buddies, powered by 'LPU Vertos,' bridges the gap between individuals facing mobility challenges and nearby volunteers eager to assist. Whether it's attending classes or completing errands, users can easily request support and connect with willing Vertos within their Campus. Through features like real-time communication and accessibility options, we're cultivating a culture of inclusivity and mutual aid, empowering individuals to achieve greater independence and mobility."

Our Vision

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is proud to be an inclusive institution that welcomes everyone, regardless of their background. Students from all over India and international students can join LPU to be part of its diverse and dynamic community. The university is dedicated to providing equal opportunities to all students, including those with disabilities and mobility impairments. LPU offers support and accommodations to these students by providing accessible facilities and specialized services. To further enhance their university experience, LPU has introduced the Helping Buddies website, which assists students with disabilities and mobility impairments as they navigate the vast 700-acre campus. This platform ensures that these students can easily access the resources and support they need to excel academically and socially.
